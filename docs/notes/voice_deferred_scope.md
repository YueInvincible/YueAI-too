# Voice Deferred Scope

## Muc dich

Giu lai pham vi voice da tung duoc nhac, nhung danh dau ro la chua phai uu tien hien tai.

## Trang thai

- Voice dang o trang thai defer.
- Khong nen lam song song voi desktop bridge/runtime neu chua khoa duong chay co ban.

## Y tuong cu can giu

- STT co the nghieng ve huong local, co VAD/push-to-talk.
- TTS can duoc benchmark theo nhom giai phap nhe, khoi dong nhanh, de huy.
- Can co co che cancel/interruption tot neu tro lai lam voice.

## Dieu khong duoc tu coi la xong

- Khong co benchmark latency.
- Khong co xac nhan chat luong Tieng Viet.
- Khong co proof ve interrupt/cancel.
- Khong co proof voice pipeline chay on dinh cung desktop app.

## Khi nao nen quay lai

- Sau khi desktop non-dev path on dinh.
- Sau khi runtime/model co huong ro hon.
- Sau khi permissions va observer policy duoc dinh nghia ro.
